Who We Are

Little Kids Rock provides instruments, curriculum and professional development to K-12 and collegiate educators. Our work transforms lives by restoring, expanding and innovating music programs in public schools across the country. Together, our teachers have brought the transformational gift of music education to over 650,000 US public school children.

K-12 Teachers

You can bring the power and passion of popular music to your school! Little Kids Rock prepares K-12 school music teachers and general education classroom teachers to offer Modern Band programs at their schools. We provide professional development, curricular resources, musical instruments, programmatic support and an expanded peer network. Our network of nearly 2,000 schoolteachers brings music to over 280,000 students each week.

Colleges & Universities

Little Kids Rock is the original pioneer of “Modern Band” in the United States public school system. The increasing presence of Modern Band and popular music education at the primary and secondary levels presents forward-looking music teacher education programs with an excellent opportunity to fully prepare their students to teach in the classrooms of tomorrow. As a leader in the field of culturally relevant, inclusive and student-centered music education, we partner with colleges and universities across the country to help share best practices, develop leadership and create sustainable coursework in popular music education approaches.
